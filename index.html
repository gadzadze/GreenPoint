Our Goal

GreenPoint was founded in 2024 with a simple goal: to make electric vehicle charging accessible to everyone while creating economic opportunities for charger hosts.

We believe that the transition to electric vehicles shouldn't be limited by charging infrastructure. By leveraging existing private charging stations, we're building the world's largest decentralized EV charging network.

Our platform connects EV owners with hosts who have charging stations, creating a win-win situation for both parties and accelerating the adoption of sustainable transportation.
